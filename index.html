Welcome

I am a Senior Applied Scientist at Amazon AWS AI HLT group in Seattle. Recently, my research has focused on data and computational efficiency of language models, output representations, efficient attention, and streamlining pretrained models. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Washington with Prof. Noah A Smith funded by SNSF and at the Idiap Research Institute with Dr. James Henderson funded by the European Union. Before that, I earned my doctoral degree at EPFL in Switzerland working with Prof. Andrei Popescu-Belis.
