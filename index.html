Welcome

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering department of the University of Washington working with Professor Noah Smith. My current research on improving the sample and computational efficiency of language models is funded by SNSF. Previously, I did a postdoc with Doctor James Henderson at the Idiap Research Institute's natural language understanding group and earned my doctoral degree at EPFL with Professor Andrei Popescu-Belis. I have also interned at Columbia University and received my master's and engineer's degrees from the University of the Aegean.
